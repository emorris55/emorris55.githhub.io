PROJECT
Arcadia is fueled by my daydreams of the "perfect" music festival. No longer did I want these ideas trapped in my head. Thus, Arcadia Music Festival was born. I focussed on creating unique experience and establishing a brand that is known throughout the world and is synonymous with music festival culture. The mission is to continue to, inspire new ideas and to celebrate life with other festival goers while they get a unique experience with their best friends, all while bonding over the joy of music & nature.
