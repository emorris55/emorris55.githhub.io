NOT IN

PROJECT
Not In Our Town was is a movement of individuals who want to create positive change in their communities and increase tolerance and understanding of others. The goal of this campaign is to ignite the fire inside of each and every viewer. As we attempt to end the vicious cycle where comfort grows while agitation raises from knowing that you ought to be doing something, Whether it be standing up to a bully or making a change within yourself “today is the day”. This spark is fueled by passion. Perhaps your passion is linked to improving something you love to do. Perhaps contributing to making a difference in our community. Following your passion will spark the fires of transformation.
