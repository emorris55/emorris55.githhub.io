DESIGN STREAK

PROJECT
This logo is inspired by the journey that a caterpillar must undergo in order to transform into a graceful butterfly. Design streak acts as a platform that allows design students to manifest new behaviors and transform into the next phase of our lives. The pattern represents that of a monarch butterfly, The orange color sparks passion, creativity, and offers emotional strength during difficult times. As each of us transforms through multiple stages of our careers.
