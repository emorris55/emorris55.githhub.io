PROJECT
For this project I aim to expose the oppression and cultural appropriation that the highlanders endured due to a systematic domination from english culture. I intended to do so by creating a co-operative board game of culture and pride. In each game the dominant culture of England intends on eradicating highland culture. Each player will take on the role of different clan chief and together try to defend heir way of life. As the game progressâ€™s english influence builds, and more laws are put into place. The goal of this game is to spark a conversation as players contemplate the losses and celebrate their successes.
